Pong

Play ping-pong. Two players control a moving ball with paddles.
If it goes past the edge of the window on the left or right, the game ends.

Press the space bar to start.
Press Q to quit.
Player 1:
  "i": move up
  "k": stop/start
  ",": move down

Player 2:
  "keypad 8": move up
  "keypad 5": stop/start
  "keypad 2": move down
